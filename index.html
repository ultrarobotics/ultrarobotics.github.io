Fabio Boekel

Robotics Engineer

Hello! I am Fabio, and I recently graduated with a Master's in (Cognitive) Robotics from TU Delft. I am passionate about robotics, from conceptual design to the intricacies of mechanical and electrical engineering, and the advanced cognitive software that enables robots to function effectively in unstructured environments. My expertise lies in bridging these areas, possessing a broad knowledge base while specializing in cognitive software solutions, including computer vision, robot control, learning, reasoning, and planning. I am adept at mechatronics prototyping, creating aesthetic designs, and translating them into functional systems. Currently, my focus is on learning-based design and control applied to legged robots.
