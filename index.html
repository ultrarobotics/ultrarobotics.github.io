Fabio Boekel

Robotics Engineer

I am passionate about robotics, from conceptual design to the intricacies of mechanical and electrical engineering, and the advanced cognitive software which makes robots work well in unstructured environments. My expertise lies in bridging these areas, possessing a broad knowledge base while specializing in cognitive software solutions, which includes computer vision, robot control, learning, reasoning, and planning. I am adept at mechatronics prototyping, creating aesthetic designs, and translating them into functional systems. Currently my focus is on learning-based design and control applied to legged robots. I am a team player, eager to learn from others and enthusiastic about sharing my knowledge.
